Product Brief

STING, the flagship product of Valtrix Systems, is a bare-metal software system for design verification of SoC implementations. Implemented in an architecture agnostic manner, it supports generation of constrained random, directed or coverage-based portable stimulus for multiple IPs. Tests can be seamlessly reused across pre-silicon simulations, FPGA prototypes, emulation and silicon. Click on the link below for more details.
